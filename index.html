Building hardware that

I'm an Embedded Hardware Design Engineer interning at PSYC Aerospace and Defence Industries in Bengaluru — designing custom PCBs for aerospace, robotics, and defence platforms. My work spans schematic capture, multilayer layout, component selection, bring-up, soldering, and validation.

Earlier I worked on analog IC design at IIIT Kottayam, simulating CMOS VCOs in Cadence Virtuoso. My projects range from 4-layer BLDC motor controllers to 6-layer UAV flight computers, compact winch controllers, EEG acquisition hardware, and security/deterrent systems.

I thrive where precision electronics meets real-world systems — where a routed trace becomes a motor spinning, a sensor reading, or a drone flying. Every board I ship is designed to IPC standards with production release in mind.
